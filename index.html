Listening test : differences between violins

Thank you for taking part in this listening test!

The rules are simple: in each round you will have to listen to two violin recordings. You will have to judge the difference between the two violins being played, regardless of the musical interpretation. You will use a ten-value scale ranging from "not at all different" (very similar, in other words) to "very different".

Press X or Y to listen to a recording. Press again to pause. Use the slider to rate the difference between the violins, then move on to the next round by clicking on the arrow at the bottom right.

Good luck!
